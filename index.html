Welcome

Hi, I’m Gabrielle Gamboa, a full‑stack developer, analytics strategist, and lifelong learner who loves building accessible, responsive, and data‑driven digital experiences.

My career has taken me across web development, application design, and large‑scale analytics. Along the way, I’ve grown into someone who can move comfortably between UX, engineering, and data, designing tools that are both intuitive and technically sound.

This portfolio is the first time I’ve brought my work together in one place, and I’m excited to share the projects, systems, and ideas that have shaped my journey across industries like healthcare, education, and government.

About Me

I bring a blend of technical precision, UX sensitivity, and operational insight to every project. Whether I’m optimizing a landing page, building a custom workflow tool, or designing ADA‑compliant interfaces, I approach each challenge with curiosity, clarity, and a commitment to meaningful impact.

I’ve always been drawn to the intersection of design and engineering, the space where thoughtful interfaces meet reliable, scalable systems. That’s where I do my best work.

Why I Build

My degree in Computer Information Systems opened the door to a career that has pushed me to grow as a developer, analyst, designer, and collaborator. Each role has taught me something new about how I think, how I solve problems, and how much I enjoy creating tools that make people’s work easier.

I build because I love the process, the debugging, the iteration, the “aha” moments, the late‑night breakthroughs. When you genuinely enjoy what you do, the work becomes energizing, whether you’re in a fast‑paced environment or deep in a complex project.

Technology is my medium, but people are my purpose. I build to empower, connect, and improve lives through thoughtful, well‑designed solutions.
