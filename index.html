About Me

Hi, I’m Gabrielle Gamboa, a full-stack developer, analytics strategist, and lifelong learner with a passion for building accessible, responsive, and data-driven web experiences.

Over the past six years, I’ve worked across web and application development, with my career expanding into big data and analytics. That journey led me to focus more deeply on strategic reporting and dashboarding, while continuing to build digital tools that solve real-world problems.

This is my first formal portfolio, and I’m excited to finally showcase the work I’ve done and the skills I’ve honed across industries like healthcare, education, and government.
I bring a unique blend of technical precision, UX sensitivity, and operational insight to every project. Whether it’s optimizing a landing page, building a custom WordPress site, or designing ADA-compliant interfaces, I approach each challenge with curiosity, collaboration, and a commitment to impact.

Thanks for visiting, more projects and updates coming soon!

Why I Build

My degree in Computer Information Systems opened doors I never imagined, not just into IT, but into roles that challenged me to grow as a developer, analyst, designer, and collaborator. Each opportunity has helped me discover how detail-oriented and creative I truly am, and how fulfilling it is to build tools that make a difference.

I build because I love the process. Whether I’m troubleshooting a deployment, designing a dashboard, or writing clean code, I’m energized by the challenge and the impact. When you love what you do, it doesn’t feel like work — even in fast-paced environments or complex projects. That mindset has carried me through every pivot, every late-night fix, and every breakthrough.

Technology is my medium, but people are my purpose. I build to empower, connect, and improve lives — one solution at a time.
